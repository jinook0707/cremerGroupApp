Cremer group in IST (Institute of Science and Technology) Austria study the behavioural, chemical and immunological mechanisms of individual and social immunity in ants, and how they affect epidemiology in the colony and ultimately colony fitness.
CremerGroupApp is a launcher to start apps developed in Cremer group for various research projects.
The CremerGroupApp repository [link] will include all the scripts and programs, developed to facilitate research on social immunity of ants in Cremer group, will be uploaded to the repository.
