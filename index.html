Cremer group in IST (Institute of Science and Technology) Austria [Group homepage link] study the behavioural, chemical and immunological mechanisms of individual and social immunity in ants, and how they affect epidemiology in the colony and ultimately colony fitness.
This is a Github-Pages site for CremerGroupApp, which is a launcher to start apps developed in Cremer group for various research projects.
The CremerGroupApp repository [link] will include all the scripts and programs, developed to facilitate research in the group.

This site will also contain web-based contents for the group such as interactive figures.
